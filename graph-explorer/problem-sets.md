## Recommended Study Order

1. **BFS & DFS** — Foundation (start here)
2. **Topological Sort** — Builds on DFS
3. **Union-Find** — Essential data structure
4. **Dijkstra** — Shortest path with weights
5. **Bellman-Ford** — Negative weights
6. **MST (Kruskal/Prim)** — Uses Union-Find
7. **Tarjan's / Kosaraju's** — Advanced DFS
8. **Floyd-Warshall** — All-pairs
9. **Network Flow** — Advanced
10. **A* / Bidirectional BFS** — Optimization
